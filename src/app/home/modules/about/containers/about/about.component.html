NG-BE 2023

NG-BE is the Belgian Angular conference and will be held at the beautiful Holiday Inn Ghent, Expo venue in Ghent on March 23 & 24, 2023.

Workshop day

March 23 is a multi-track workshop day, where attendees can learn about the latest Angular developments.

More details? Click here for our full workshop planning

Conference day

March 24 is a single-track conference day. Attendees will be able to listen to talks from world-renowned Angular experts while enjoying delicious foods and drinks.
